Tasty Herb Quark Recipe



Description:

Very delicious and super easy to cook recipes for a vegan herb quark.



Ingredients:

  • 400 g vegan quark
  • 200 g vegan natural yogurt
  • 0.5 red onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 15 g fresh herbs (e.g. chives, parsley, basil, ...)
  • fresh lemon juice
  • salt and pepper

Preparation:

  1. Mix the vegan quark and yogurt in a large bowl until smooth. Peel and finely chop the onion and garlic. Wash the fresh herbs, chop finely too and add to the bowl together with the onion and garlic. Stir the herb quark well and season with lemon juice, salt and pepper.
